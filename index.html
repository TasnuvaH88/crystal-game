Crystals Collector Game!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. Each crystal has a number assigned to it. Every time you click on one, it will add that value to your total score.
The number of each crystal is hidden from you, so you need to figure out the right combination to match your score to the randomized number.
You will win when the numbers match, and lose if your number goes over. The values of each crystal change with each game!
